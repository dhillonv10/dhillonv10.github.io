**I've talked about continous deployment and integration before but today I wanna talk about one of the providers [Codeship](https://www.codeship.io).** The service is an absolute pleasure to use and their support is just as good. Let's look at why you'd want to use Codeship: 

1. Easily integrates into your github
2. **Free builds for open source projects - always**
3. **100 builds, no charge each month for your personal projects**
4. Amazing support and easy to understand terms of service - A huge plus for me. 
5. You get **developer swag** - Another big one

So I just wanted to take a moment and say thanks to Codeship for being amazing and send me some free developer swag!!
