**You just need two minutes to think of an idea, but what does it take to actually create a company around that idea? A startup is a lot more than just an idea.**

1. If you think you have a decent idea, spend just 2 minutes internally fleshing out the idea.
2. If still think its a good idea, give it 2 hours to more thoroughly brainstorm and consider the tradeoffs.
3. If you still think&nbsp;its a good idea, give it 2 days.&nbsp; At this point, you might want to get other people involved for consultation.
4. Still? Give it 2 weeks. At this point you want to be fairly serious about coming up with a pitch and prototyping the product.
5. Finally, 2 months. At the end of 2 months, you want to be relatively sure you are fully committed and can make this into a business.

This progressive strategy for committing time allows you to explore new ideas while also constantly re-evaluating if the project is going to be successful and worthy of more time.
