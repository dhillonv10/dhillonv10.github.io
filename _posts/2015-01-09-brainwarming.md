**Brain-warming instead of brainstorming**

There has not been any definitive reseach in learning methods that comparitively shows whether brainstorming works or not. It has been our go-to method to start thinking about a topic and we have been taught the similar variations for all sorts of standardized testing. Recently, Tony McCaffrey (@DrTonyMcCaffrey) and their group have been looking more into learning techniques and they discovered that brainstorming actually hurts creativity instead of helping it. Their group researches and develops innovation tools at Innovation Accelerator, Inc. Brainswarming is the latest tool to emerge from his research and will soon become an online platform for remote group work. Have a look:
